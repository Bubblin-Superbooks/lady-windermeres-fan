Third Act

Scene

Lord Darlington’s Rooms. A large sofa is in front of fireplace R.
At the back of the stage a curtain is drawn across the window. Doors L. and R.
Table R. with writing materials. Table C. with syphons, glasses, and Tantalus frame. Table L. with cigar and cigarette box. Lamps lit.
